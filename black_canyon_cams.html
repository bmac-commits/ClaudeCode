⚠️Rim Edge & Altitude SafetyNo guardrails▾
⚠️ NPS's own safety page leads with this: weathered rock makes rim edges hazardous, and many overlooks have no guardrails at all. Stay on marked trails and behind railings where they exist — falls are the park's defining danger. NPS is blunt about another rule: "Never throw anything from the rim into the canyon! Even a small stone can be fatal" to hikers or climbers far below. Other hazards: altitude (~8,000 ft, watch for AMS symptoms), sudden summer lightning, and ticks in moist inner-canyon areas. 🔥South Rim Fire Recovery2025 fire impact▾
🔥 A lightning-caused wildfire (the 2025 South Rim Fire, ignited July 10, 2025) burned roughly 85% of the South Rim area, damaging the South Rim Campground, entrance station area, and several trails. Recovery is ongoing — South Rim Campground is operating at reduced capacity for the 2026 season (see Campgrounds below), and the Rim Rock & Uplands Trails pass through heavily burned terrain with uneven surfaces and blowing ash. Warner Point Trail and all North Rim trails are open with no fire impact. NPS fire status page ↗
🪧Park Access & FeesSouth Rim 24/7 · $30▾
South Rim Open 24/7 Year-Round
Paved access via CO-347, from Montrose
The main, most-visited side — paved road, the Visitor Center, South Rim Road overlooks, and (reduced-capacity) South Rim Campground.
💡 Open around the clock, every day of the year — no seasonal closure on this side.
NPS directions ↗
North Rim Seasonal · Gravel
Unpaved gravel road, via CO-92 near Crawford
The last 7 miles of the access road and all of North Rim Drive are unpaved. Road and ranger station typically close mid-November, reopening early May.
💡 No road connects the two rims inside the park — driving between them takes 2+ hours around via public highways.

Black Canyon of the Gunnison was designated an International Dark Sky Park on September 8, 2015. The canyon's dramatic depth and remote high-elevation setting make South Rim and North Rim overlooks excellent viewing spots, far from city lights. NPS night sky info ↗
