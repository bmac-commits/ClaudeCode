⚠️Cave & Trail SafetyUneven, wet trails▾
🕳️ Cave trails are uneven, often wet, and pass through sections with low ceilings and dim lighting — use handrails and stay on marked paths. Cave temperature holds steady at 54°F year-round, so bring a light jacket even in summer. On the surface, watch for ticks, poison ivy, and venomous copperheads and rattlesnakes along trails and near the Green River. Summer heat can exceed 90°F — carry water on any outdoor hike. Children under 16 must be supervised at all times on tours. NPS safety info ↗
🎟️Cave Tours & ReservationsBook ahead▾
📅 The surface and visitor center are free, but every cave tour requires a paid, timed ticket — advance reservations via Recreation.gov are strongly recommended, since popular tours sell out, especially in summer and around holidays. Ranger-led tours range from easy walk-throughs to strenuous crawling routes. NPS tour guide ↗
